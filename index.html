Sadqa (charity) Islam mein ek bahut ahem amal hai aur iski fazilat QURAN-E-PAK aur HADEES-E-PAK mein bayan ki gayi hai. Sadqa dene ka amal insaniyat aur imaniyat dono mein barakah aur khairat lekar aata hai. QURAN-E-PAK mein ALLAH Ta'ala ne irshad farmaya hai: "Jo shakhs ALLAH PAK ki rah mein maal kharch karta hai, uski misl ek bijli ki tarah hai jo saath lekar uthaye jaati hai aur andheron mein roshni pheila deti hai" (Al-Baqarah 2:261). Is ayat se zahir hota hai ke sadqa dene wale ki madad se ALLAH uski mushkilein door karta hai aur uski zindagi mein roshni bhar deta hai. Sadqa dene ka tareeqa bhi Islam mein wazeh taur par bayan kiya gaya hai. Prophet MUHAMMAD ï·º ne irshad farmaya: "Tumhare maal mein (sawab hasil karne) ka sabse acha tareeqa sadqa karna hai" (Sahih Bukhari). Is hadith se samajh aata hai ke sadqa dene se insan apne maal ka paish-e-nazar Allah ke liye karta hai aur isse uski nafarmaniyan maaf ho jati hain. Sadqa dene ke fayde sirf duniya mein hi nahi, balki akhirat mein bhi hote hain. ALLAH PAK ne un logon ko jannat ki basharat di hai jo apne maal ko kharch karke dusron ki madad karte hain. QURAN-E-PAK mein farmaya gaya hai: "ALLAH PAK jo shakhs apne maal aur apni jaan ko Allah ki rah mein kharch karta hai, unke liye unki Rab ki taraf se unki mukhlis dua hai aur unke liye bada ajr hai" (Al-Baqarah 2: 261). Sadqa dene se gareebon aur muhtaajon ki madad hoti hai, aur iske zariye samaj mein ek dostana mahaul banta hai. Yeh amal insaniyat, daya aur muhabbat ka izhar karta hai. Iske alawa, sadqa dene wale ki rozi mein barkat hoti hai aur uske maal ka paish-e-nazar ho jata hai. Is taur par, sadqa dene ka amal Islam mein bada ahem hai, jise amal karke insan not only apne duniyawi masail se nijat pa sakta hai balki akhirat mein bhi badi khushiyan hasil kar sakta hai.
