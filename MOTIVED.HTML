Saylani Trust Pakistan ek welfare organization hai jo ke 1999 mein Karachi, Pakistan mein Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui ki taraf se banai gayi thi. Is trust ka maqsad ghareebon aur mohtaaj logon ki madad karna hai, unhe khana, pehnawa, aur medical facilities faraham karna hai. Saylani Trust ka maqsad darasal ek aisa samaj banana hai jahan har shakhs be rozgar, be ghar aur be maqsad logon ki madad kar sake. Is trust ne mulk bhar mein madad karne ke liye aala taleem, sehat, aur ghareebon ki zarooriyaat mein madad faraham karne ke liye amal kiya hai. Saylani Trust ke zariye rozgar, ghar, aur khana faraham karke, yateemon, mohtaajon, aur ghareebon ki madad ki ja rahi hai. Iske ilawa, education, healthcare, aur emergency relief mein bhi is trust ne apni khidmaten di hain. Saylani Trust ka tajaweez karda model hai ke ek organization kis tarah se apne asoolon par qaim reh kar, mukhtalif shobon mein ghareebon ki madad kar sakti hai. Isne apni taqat aur resources ko istemal kar ke samaji tabdeeli laane ka maqsad rakha hai.
